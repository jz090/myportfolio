Overview

A group of six Penn State students form the team to create an event posting platform which intended for college users. I joined the team as UX/UI designer and designed the iOS app. I'm currently designing the web page for Festa. It will be up on my portfolio as soon as I complete it.

Please check out the design detail below:

Project Objective & Persona

The product objective is to provide a platform (Events Hub) for school clubs/ event organizations or students to post and join events.

Design

Throughtout the the design, I used "Avenir" font and orange + white for theme color; this will create relax and proactive feeling for users. Also I used rounded square to represent channel, circle for user profile, and rectangle for events.
