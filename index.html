About Me

Hi, I graduated from The Pennsylvania State University with B.S. degree in Information Sciences and Technology. I am passionate about Creative Thinking and Problem Solving. I'm constantly learning web technologies and other design related topics.

I've had experiences in IT Consulting, UI Design and Programming. I've worked at FAA as Integration Engineer; Accenture as QA Tester; Penn State IT Service Desk as IT Supervisor. In my free time, I've worked on variety of projects, such as 2D Game Design, Logo Design, Mobile App Design and Magic Releases.

Fun fact, I'm also a magic creator, I have created and published orginal magic tricks.
